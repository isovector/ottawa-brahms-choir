In memory of Elisabeth Salm, 1959-2018

The memorial gathering for Elisabeth Salm took place on Friday, June 1st, 7:00 at the First Unitarian Congregation of Ottawa, 30 Cleary Ave, Ottawa, K2A 4A1.

Obituary Ottawa Citizen...

Obituary: SALM, Elisabeth Christina. The core values of service, radical inclusiveness, compassion, and grace make the healing impact of the life of Elisabeth Christina Salm unstoppable.
 
"Djies" was born in Hawkesbury, Ontario on January 21, 1959 - the second of five children. Her father, Jacob Salm, originally from Haarlem, the Netherlands, was greatly influenced by the pioneering plant ecologist and teacher, Jac. P. Thijsse. 
(Cont...)

Elisabeth, November 29th 2015, when she spoke about clean energy at the Ottawa Brahms Choir Christmas Concert.

The Ottawa Brahms Choir singing Brahms's "Selig sind, die da Leid tragen" at the memorial gathering for Elisabeth Salm on Friday, June 1st, at the First Unitarian Congregation of Ottawa,

Elisabeth Salm was an active member of the Ottawa Brahms Choir from 2010 – 2018 and sang in most concerts. Elisabeth was Secretary of the Ottawa Brahms Choir Board since the Fall of 2015 and had agreed at the AGM on April 31, 2018 to take on the position of Treasurer.
